About NK Facts

A podcast born from curiosity, crafted with love — exploring the beautiful, strange, and fascinating facts about our world.

Who We Are

Storytelling meets curiosity

NK Facts was created with one simple mission: to make learning about the world around us as delightful as possible. We believe that facts don't have to be dry — they can be magical, surprising, and deeply moving.

Every episode is researched with care, narrated with warmth, and designed to leave you with that wonderful feeling of knowing something new about the universe you live in.

Whether you're taking a walk, cooking dinner, or winding down for the night — NK Facts is your perfect companion.
